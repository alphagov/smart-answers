##Student finance

You could get per year:

- £6,000 [Tuition Fee Loan](/studentfinance/continuing-fulltime-students) to pay for your course

- £3,818 [Maintenance Loan](/studentfinance/continuing-fulltime-students) to pay living costs

- £1,494 [Maintenance Grant](/studentfinance/continuing-fulltime-students) (for living costs)

###Extra student funding

You don’t qualify for extra grants and allowances.
