Will you pass through UK border control?

You pass through border control if you do any of the following:

  • leave the main airport building for any reason
  • collect your bags and check them in to your onward flight
  • travel on to the Republic of Ireland, the Isle of Man or the Channel Islands

You also pass through border control if both:

  • your onward flight leaves on a different day to when you arrive
  • there’s nowhere for you to stay overnight in the airport, eg in a transit hotel

Contact your airline if you’re not sure if you’ll pass through border control.
