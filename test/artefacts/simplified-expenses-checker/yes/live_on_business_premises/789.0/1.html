Simplified expenses

Total to claim per year:

  • £4,200 to deduct from your total business costs - you then claim the balance

What to do for your tax return

Use the simplified expenses flat rates to work out your business expenses.

Multiply the flat rates with the miles you drove, hours you work from home or the number of people who live on your business premises.

Self Assessment tax returns

Calculating expenses by working out the actual costs

Total to claim per year:

  • £789 to deduct from your total business costs - you then claim the balance

What to do for your tax return

You’ll have to work out the split between private and business use for vehicles, working from home or business premises use.

You’ll then have to add up your expenses for your tax return and keep records for every expense you want to claim, eg copies of your bills.
