Marriage in St Martin

Contact the relevant local authorities in St Martin to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.



^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) and check the [travel advice for St Martin](/foreign-travel-advice/st-martin) before making any plans.


There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in St Martin.
