Marriage in Cote d'Ivoire

Contact the relevant local authorities in Cote d'Ivoire to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.


^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) before making any plans.^


There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in Cote d'Ivoire.


*[GRO]:General Register Office
*[FCO]:Foreign &amp; Commonwealth Office
