Marriage in Cote d'Ivoire


Contact your [nearest embassy or consulate](/government/publications/foreign-embassies-in-the-uk) representing Cote d'Ivoire before making any plans to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.


^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) and check the [travel advice for Cote d'Ivoire](/foreign-travel-advice/cote-d-ivoire) before making any plans.


##Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to [become a British citizen](/becoming-a-british-citizen/if-your-spouse-is-a-british-citizen) once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
