Getting married abroad

Civil partnership in Ecuador

Same-sex relationships in Ecuador that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as ‘unión civil’, or ‘civil union’.

Contact the Embassy of Ecuador before making any plans to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.

You should also check the travel advice for Ecuador.

What you need to do

You may be asked to provide a certificate of no impediment (CNI) or a similar document to prove you’re allowed to enter into a civil partnership or equivalent in Ecuador.

Contact the local British embassy or consulate where you’re planning the ceremony to find out what you need to do.

Your partner can apply to become a British citizen once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
