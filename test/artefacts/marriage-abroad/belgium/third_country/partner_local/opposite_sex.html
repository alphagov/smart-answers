Getting married abroad

Marriage in Belgium

Contact the relevant local authorities in Belgium to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.

You should get legal advice and check the travel advice for Belgium before making any plans.

What you need to do

You may be asked to provide a certificate of no impediment (CNI) or a similar document to prove you’re allowed to marry.

There are 2 ways you can get a CNI:

  • go to the UK and post notice with a UK registrar
  • go to Belgium and post notice at the embassy or consulate there

If you choose to post notice in Belgium, you’ll normally have to wait at least 10 days before you can get married.
