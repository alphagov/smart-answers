Getting married abroad

Civil partnership in the Czech Republic

Same-sex relationships in the Czech Republic that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as ’registrované partnerství’, or ‘registered partnership’.

Contact the Embassy of the Czech Republic before making any plans to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.

You should also check the travel advice for the Czech Republic.

Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to become a British citizen once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
