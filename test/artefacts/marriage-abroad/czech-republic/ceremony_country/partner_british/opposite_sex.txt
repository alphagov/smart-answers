Marriage in the Czech Republic

Contact the relevant local authorities in the Czech Republic to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.



^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) before making any plans.^


There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in the Czech Republic.



*[GRO]:General Register Office
*[FCO]:Foreign &amp; Commonwealth Office
