You may be able to get ESA while abroad.

Contact the [International Pension Centre](/international-pension-centre) to get ESA.

You may be able to get benefits from Austria if you've been paying into a local social security insurance scheme.  Contact the local authorities to find out more.

*[ESA]: Employment Support Allowance
