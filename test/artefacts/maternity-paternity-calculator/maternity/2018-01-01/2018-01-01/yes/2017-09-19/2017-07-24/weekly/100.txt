How many payments did the employee receive between Tuesday, 25 July 2017 and Tuesday, 19 September 2017?



If you have paid the employee 2 weeks’ pay in one week, count it as 2 payments. 
Don't count bonuses or holiday payments on top of paid annual leave.

  * 8: 8 payments or fewer
  * 9: 9 payments
  * 10: 10 payments
