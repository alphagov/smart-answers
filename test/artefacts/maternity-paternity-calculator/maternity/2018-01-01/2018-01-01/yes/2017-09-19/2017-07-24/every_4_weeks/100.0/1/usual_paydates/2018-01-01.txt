

##Statutory Maternity Leave

The employee is entitled to up to 52 weeks Statutory Maternity Leave.

|Leave                         | Key dates                    |
|------------------------------|------------------------------|
|Start                         |  1 January 2018          |
|End                           | 30 December 2018            |
|Latest date to [claim leave](/maternity-leave-pay-employees/notice-period "Notice period")    | 23 September 2017  |
|Earliest date leave can start | 15 October 2017 |

##Statutory maternity pay

The employee is not entitled to statutory maternity pay.
Their average weekly earnings are £25 (you can’t round this figure up or down). To qualify:

+ their average weekly earnings (£25) between Tuesday, 25 July 2017 and Tuesday, 19 September 2017 must be at least £113

You must write confirming this within 28 days of their maternity pay request.

Send them form SMP1 within 7 days of your decision. They must get this form within 28 days of their pay request or the birth (whichever is earlier).
