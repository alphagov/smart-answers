

##Statutory Paternity Leave

The employee is entitled to Statutory Paternity Leave.

|Leave                         | Key dates                    |
|------------------------------|------------------------------|
|Start                         | 1 January 2018          |
|End                           | 7 January 2018            |
|Latest date to [claim leave](/employers-paternity-pay-leave/adoption)     | 23 September 2017  |

##Statutory Paternity Pay

The employee is not entitled to Statutory Paternity Pay. To qualify:

+ their average weekly earnings (£12.50) between Thursday, 2 November 2017 and Monday, 1 January 2018 must be at least £113

Send them form SPP1 confirming this within 28 days of their pay request.
