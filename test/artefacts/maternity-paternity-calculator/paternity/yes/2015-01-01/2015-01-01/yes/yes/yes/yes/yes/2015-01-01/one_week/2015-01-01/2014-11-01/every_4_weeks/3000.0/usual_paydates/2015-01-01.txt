

##Statutory Paternity Leave

The employee is entitled to Statutory Paternity Leave.

|Leave                         | Key dates                    |
|------------------------------|------------------------------|
|Start                         |  1 January 2015          |
|End                           |  8 January 2015            |
|Latest date to [claim leave](/employers-paternity-pay-leave/adoption)     | 20 September 2014  |

^Leave must finish within 56 days of the birth (or due date if the baby is early).^


##Statutory Paternity Pay (SPP)

The employee is entitled to SPP.

Their average weekly earnings are: £375.00
